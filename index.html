Frequently Asked Questions

Intelligence Briefing

What is ZEN ASSETS and how does it work?
ZEN ASSETS is an autonomous capital intelligence system operating across five sovereign financial corridors — New York, London, Edinburgh, Shanghai, and Hong Kong. Our deployment infrastructure generates base yield continuously through multi-vector strategies, while the quantitative engine executes thousands of optimized positions daily across crypto, equities, and yield protocols. Returns range from 55–400% APY depending on your allocation node. Initialize a position, select your node, fund it, and the deployment infrastructure operates autonomously on your behalf.
How are these returns possible when banks offer less than 5%?
Banks lend your capital at 6–12% and return 0.01–4.5% to you. Brokerages charge fees on trades you execute yourself. ZEN operates fundamentally differently: our sovereign corridor infrastructure generates continuous baseline yield independent of market direction. The autonomous engine then compounds that yield through quantitative deployment and multi-vector strategies across 120+ instruments simultaneously. This triple-layer architecture (corridor yield + quantitative alpha + yield infrastructure) is why our returns structurally exceed any single strategy.
What is the minimum allocation?
The minimum allocation depends on your chosen node: Node I (Bronze) starts at $2,000, Node II (Silver) at $25,000, Node III (Gold) at $100,000, Node IV (Platinum) at $500,000, and Node V (Diamond) at $2,000,000. Even the minimum Node I deployment of $2,000 benefits from full sovereign corridor infrastructure. Many participants begin at lower nodes and advance as deployment results compound.
Is my money safe?
Your security is our top priority. We use 256-bit SSL encryption, bcrypt password hashing, two-factor email verification (OTP) for every login, rate-limiting to prevent brute force attacks, and SOC-2 compliant infrastructure. All investments are held in segregated accounts.
How do I fund my account?
After registration, you can fund your account via cryptocurrency deposits. Your account balance starts at $0 and is only credited once funds are received and confirmed. All deposit details and wallet addresses are available in your dashboard after login.
Can I withdraw my funds anytime?
Yes. ZEN ASSETS operates with zero lockup periods. You can request a withdrawal at any time through your dashboard. Withdrawals are processed within 1-3 business days after approval. You will receive email confirmations at each step.
How do I contact support?
You can reach our support team via the live chat widget (click "Chat with Manager" on this page), or email us at thezenassets@gmail.com. Gold tier and above members receive dedicated account managers with priority response times.
