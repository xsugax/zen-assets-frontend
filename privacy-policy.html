Privacy Policy

ZEN ASSETS - Professional Stock Trading & Market Analysis Platform

1. Introduction

ZEN ASSETS ("we," "us," "our," or "Company") respects the privacy of our users ("user" or "you"). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you visit our website zenassets.tech and use our trading platform.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Information You Provide

  • Account Information: When you create an account, we collect your email, username, and other account credentials
  • Profile Information: Investment preferences, trading experience level, and portfolio data
  • Trading Data: Your trading history, orders, and market analysis preferences
  • Communication: Any messages, feedback, or support requests you send us

2.2 Information Collected Automatically

  • Device Information: Browser type, IP address, operating system
  • Usage Data: Pages visited, time spent, charts viewed, features used
  • Cookies & Tracking: Session cookies for security and performance optimization
  • Performance Data: System performance metrics and error logs (non-personal)

3. How We Use Your Information

  • Provide and improve our trading platform
  • Authenticate your account and maintain security
  • Process your trading requests and transactions
  • Send service-related announcements and updates
  • Analyze platform usage to optimize performance
  • Comply with legal obligations and prevent fraud
  • Respond to support requests and feedback

4. Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures including:

  • SSL/TLS encryption for all data in transit
  • Secure password hashing and authentication
  • Regular security audits and updates
  • Restricted access to personal information
  • Secure session management with automatic timeouts

5. Data Retention

We retain your personal information only for as long as necessary to provide our services and comply with legal obligations. You can request deletion of your account and associated data at any time by contacting support@zenassets.tech.

6. Third-Party Services

Our platform integrates with third-party services:

  • Binance API: For real-time market data and trading information
  • Analytics Services: To understand usage patterns and improve the platform
  • Cloud Infrastructure: For secure data storage and platform reliability

We do not sell or share your personal information with third parties for marketing purposes.

7. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal information
  • Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your data
  • Opt-out of non-essential communications
  • Export your trading data

8. Cookies Policy

We use cookies for:

  • Essential Cookies: Authentication and security
  • Performance Cookies: Analytics and optimization
  • Preference Cookies: Remembering your settings

You can manage cookie preferences in your browser settings.

9. Children's Privacy

ZEN ASSETS is not intended for users under 18 years old. We do not knowingly collect information from minors. If we become aware of such collection, we will delete that information immediately.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. We will notify you of significant changes via email or through a notice on our website. Your continued use constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
